## 🎯 About this application

Culoton helps you organize your daily tasks and project files. It streamlines your desktop environment by grouping related documents and tracking your progress on routine assignments. Many users struggle to keep their workspace clean while working on multiple projects. This software fixes that problem. It runs in the background and keeps your files where you need them. You work faster because you spend less time searching for folders and apps.

## 📋 System requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run culoton:

- Windows 10 or Windows 11
- 4 GB of RAM
- 200 MB of free hard drive space
- An active internet connection for initial setup

## 🚀 Getting started

Once you open the software, the main dashboard shows your current projects. You see a list of folders on the left side of your screen. These folders act as containers for your work. 

To create a new project:

1. Click the Plus button in the top menu.
2. Name your project.
3. Choose the folder on your hard drive that contains your project files.
4. Click Save.

The software now associates those files with your project. You can access these files through the culoton interface anytime. Your settings save automatically. You do not need to click a Save button after you add a task or regroup a file.

## 🛠️ Managing your workspace

Culoton provides several ways to organize your digital workspace. You can drag and drop items to categorize them. Use the tagging system to label files by priority or project stage. 

If you work on many projects at once, use the filter bar at the top of the app. Type a keyword to narrow down your list. The app updates the view in real time.

You can also set reminders for specific tasks. Click the clock icon next to a project name to open the calendar. Pick a date and time. The app sends a notification to your Windows taskbar when the time comes. This feature helps you stay on track with important deadlines.

## ⚙️ Customizing the interface

You can change how the software looks to suit your comfort. Open the Settings menu by clicking the gear icon in the bottom left corner. 

- **Display**: Switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode. Dark Mode reduces eye strain if you work late at night.
- **Start-up**: Choose if the app should open automatically when you start your computer. 
- **Storage**: Change where the app stores its internal data if you reach your current drive capacity.

These changes apply immediately. Click the back arrow to return to your workspace.

## 🔒 Privacy and security

Your data stays on your computer. Culoton does not send your files or personal project details to an external server. The app acts as a bridge between your eyes and the files already sitting on your hard drive. 

Because the app operates locally, you can use it without an internet connection. Your security remains high because no outside entity accesses your drive. If you decide to uninstall the app, your files remain in their original folders. The app only removes its own configuration and link files.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**Does this app slow down my computer?**
No. The app uses a small amount of memory. It stays dormant until you click the icon to open it.

**Can I move my project folders after I add them?**
Yes. If you move a folder, a red warning icon appears next to the project name in the app. Click the warning, and the app will ask you to select the new location of the folder. After you re-link the folder, everything works as before.

**What happens if I delete a project in the app?**
Deleting a project from the culoton list does not delete your files on your computer. It only removes the link within the app. Your work stays safe in its original folder.

**How do I update the software?**
The app checks for updates when you open it. If a new version exists, a notification appears at the top of the interface. Click the update button to install the latest version automatically.
